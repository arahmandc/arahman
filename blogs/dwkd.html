    Bangladesh is a land of rivers and geomorphologically a delta region. A larger part of our population relies on their day to day survival on the water! To embrace the #SDG goals, we also need a sustainable water management plan and its implementation for Bangladesh. The Kingdom of Netherlands Government has partnered with Bangladesh Government to strengthen the water management capacity of Bangladesh and to be prepared for the climate change challenges! In response to that Dhaka Water Knowledge Days (#DWKD) event being hosted in Bangladesh incorporating a series of similar events globally. Bangladesh Open Innovation Lab (BOIL) is proud to be a part of this journey as a knowledge development partner.
