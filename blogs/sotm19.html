    State of the Map Asia 2019 Dhaka, Bangladesh SotMAsia is the biggest conference for the mappers and the mapping enthusiastic people of this region. The Sotm Asia build the bridges between the mappers, GIS experts, developers, researchers and academics, private and public institutions. also supporting the Already organizations like Facebook, Apple, Mapbox, UNDP, GRC, BDRC, IFRC, Global Logic showed their positive interest on joining the event. EMK center is also supporting the event. SOtM Asia 2019 will be held at KIB The annual gathering of the most brightest & active map, Open data and ICT enthusiasts, contributors, Researchers, academics & developers of OpenStreetMap platform in Asia, State of the Map Asia 2019 (SotMAsia19) will be held at Krishibid Institute of Bangladesh (KIB) Dhaka, Bangladesh on 1st & 2nd November. SOtM Asia 2019 Facebook Event Asia's biggest mapper conference is coming soon to Our Dhaka City.
